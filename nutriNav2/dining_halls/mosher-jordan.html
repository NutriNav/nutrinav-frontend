Mosher-Jordan

  • 200 Observatory, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109
  • 734-764-2111
  • dining-mosherjordan@umich.edu

Situated on the Hill, Mosher-Jordan offers a superb view of Palmer Field and even better food. Frequent themed meals offer creative and fun menus for a change of pace. If you needed any more reason to go — everyone knows Mojo’s cookies are irresistible.

Menu offerings are subject to change; please check menu signage in the dining hall for the most up-to-date information.
